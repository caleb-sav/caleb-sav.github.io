Servo Class
===============
This class controls the pen-up and pen-down action of the robot. The class is 
designed for use with a standard hobby servo. It runs PWM at 50 Hz with pulse-widths 
between **[Insert Value]** and **[Insert Value]** to serve as the 'up' and 'down' 
positions.
